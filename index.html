DETERMINATION OF SENSORY PERCEPTION PROFILE

VISUAL-AUDITORY-KINESTHETIC

VISUAL

Visual learners prefer to see information presented graphically or visually, such as in diagrams, videos, or images. They tend to remember information better when it's presented visually.

AUDITORY

Auditory learners prefer to hear information presented in the form of verbal explanations, lectures, or discussions. They tend to remember information better when it's presented orally.

KINESTHETIC

Kinesthetic learners prefer to learn by doing, using practical examples or hands-on experiences. They tend to remember information better when they're physically involved in the learning process.
